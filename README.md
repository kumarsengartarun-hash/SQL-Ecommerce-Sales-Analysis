# SQL-Ecommerce-Sales-Analysis

## 📌 Project Overview
This project analyzes e-commerce sales data using MySQL to identify business trends, customer behavior, product performance, and revenue insights. The objective is to demonstrate SQL skills commonly required for Data Analyst roles.

## 🎯 Project Objectives
- Analyze overall sales performance
- Identify top-selling products
- Find high-value customers
- Analyze city-wise revenue
- Track monthly sales trends
- Generate actionable business insights

## 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
- MySQL
- SQL
- GitHub
- CSV Dataset

## 📊 Business Questions Solved

### 1. Total Revenue Analysis
Calculated total revenue generated from all sales transactions.

### 2. Top Selling Products
Identified products with the highest quantity sold.

### 3. Revenue by Product
Analyzed revenue contribution by each product.

### 4. Revenue by Category
Compared revenue generated across product categories.

### 5. Top Customers
Identified customers with the highest spending.

### 6. City-wise Revenue Analysis
Analyzed sales performance across different cities.

### 7. Monthly Revenue Trend
Tracked revenue growth month-over-month.

### 8. Average Order Value
Calculated average revenue per order.

### 9. Customer Purchase Frequency
Measured repeat purchase behavior.

## 📈 Key Insights

- Electronics category generated the highest revenue.
- Laptop was the highest revenue-generating product.
- Delhi contributed the highest overall sales.
- Top customers generated a significant portion of total revenue.
- Monthly sales showed positive growth trends.
